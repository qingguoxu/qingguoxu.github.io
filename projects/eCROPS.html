electrical Cost-effective Roll-Over Protective Structures (eCROPS)
 

Project Description

 

People who work on farms should pay attention to safety when using a truck, because the truck can roll over and kill the operator sometime. The App (eCROPS) focuses on helping high school students learn how to build roll-over protective structures on trucks and eventually, the students can help their parents build a protective structure on trucks. The App includes safety news (includes news from social media), tutorial materials, discuss forum, and etc. I designed and implemented the App.
